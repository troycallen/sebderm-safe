FAQ

What is seborrheic dermatitis?

Seborrheic dermatitis is a common skin condition that mainly affects the scalp, causing scaly patches, red skin, and stubborn dandruff. It can also occur in other oily areas of the body, such as the face, upper chest, and back.

How accurate is this tool?

While we strive for accuracy, this tool should be used as a guide only. Our database is regularly updated with the latest research, but individual reactions can vary. Always consult with a dermatologist for personalized advice.

Can I use this tool for other skin conditions?

This tool is specifically designed for seborrheic dermatitis. Other skin conditions may have different triggers and safe ingredients. We recommend using condition-specific resources for other skin issues.

How often is the ingredient database updated?

We review and update our ingredient database monthly, incorporating the latest research and findings in seborrheic dermatitis management.
