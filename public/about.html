About Us

SebDerm-Safe is a tool designed to help people with seborrheic dermatitis make informed decisions about the products they use. Our mission is to simplify the process of identifying safe and potentially problematic ingredients in skincare and haircare products.

We understand the challenges of living with seborrheic dermatitis and aim to provide a user-friendly resource for managing this condition. We will try to keep the database up-to-date with the latest findings in seborrheic dermatitis research.
